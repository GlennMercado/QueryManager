Tags and Status filter

In the sidebar, there is a button labeled 'Tags', where you can find a complete list of all available tags.
There is a section labeled 'Status' that allows you to filter posts based on their status. You can choose to display only 'Resolved', 'Unresolved', or All posts.
When the 'Resolved' filter is applied, the section will display only posts that have been marked as resolved. For example:
  • Posts where the issues have been successfully addressed or solutions have been provided.
  • Entries with a clear indication of being marked as resolved by the original poster or an administrator.
This filter helps users focus on solutions that are already available.
When the 'Unresolved' filter is applied, the section will display only posts that have not yet been resolved. For example:
  • Posts where the issue or query is still open and awaiting a solution.
  • Entries that have not been marked as resolved by the original poster or an administrator.
This filter is useful for identifying discussions or problems that require further input or assistance.

Post

When you click on the 'Post Your Query' text box, a form will appear with the following fields:
  • Attachment: Allows you to upload relevant files or documents.
  • Issue: A concise title or summary of the problem or question.
  • Detail: A detailed description of the query, including any necessary context or background information.
  • Reference Form: Optional references or sources related to the query.
  • Tags: Keywords or categories to help classify the query for better visibility and organization.
These fields are designed to ensure that your query is well-structured and easy for others to understand and respond to.
In the 'Tags' section, you can input as many tags as necessary, provided they are relevant to the post. Tags help categorize the content and make it easier for others to discover and engage with your post.
